================================================================================
                         DATABASE MODELS (7 TOTAL)
================================================================================

PARKING MODELS (5):
  1. ParkingZone
     - Purpose: Define parking areas/zones
     - Fields: name, description, is_active, created_at
     - Records: 7
     
  2. ParkingSlot
     - Purpose: Individual parking spaces
     - Fields: slot_number, status, is_occupied, zone_id, created_at
     - Status Options: available, occupied, maintenance
     - Records: 10
     - Constraint: unique_together(zone, slot_number)
     
  3. Vehicle
     - Purpose: Vehicle registration and tracking
     - Fields: license_plate, vehicle_type, owner_name, owner_phone, created_at
     - Vehicle Types: car, bike, truck
     - Records: 3
     - Constraint: unique license_plate
     
  4. ParkingSession
     - Purpose: Track parking events (entry/exit)
     - Fields: vehicle_id, slot_id, user_id, created_by_id, status, entry_time, exit_time, total_amount
     - Status Options: booked, active, completed, cancelled
     - Records: 6
     - Properties: duration_minutes (calculated)
     
  5. Payment
     - Purpose: Record payment transactions
     - Fields: session_id, payment_type, payment_method, amount, status, transaction_id, processed_by_id
     - Types: session_fee, fine, other
     - Methods: online, cash
     - Status: pending, successful, failed, partial
     - Records: 6

ANALYTICS MODELS (2):
  6. AnalyticsReport
     - Purpose: Store generated analytics reports
     - Fields: title, report_type, data (JSON), generated_by_id, created_at
     - Report Types: daily, weekly, monthly, custom
     - Records: 19
     
  7. SystemMetrics
     - Purpose: Store real-time system snapshots
     - Fields: timestamp, total_slots, occupied_slots, available_slots, daily_revenue, active_sessions
     - Records: 7
     - Status: Auto-generated, read-only
